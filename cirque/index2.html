About Us

Circus Wild is a circus company created in 1975, and since its creation several artists have performed on its stages. Circus Wild shows are intended for adults and children alike the artists who perform on the stages of the Circus Wild circus, come to us from all over the world, from France, the United States, Belgium, Germany...
